Hi! Starting in August I will be a Hale Visiting Assistant Professor and ARC Postdoctoral Fellow at Georgia Tech. My research interests lie broadly within discrete probability, high-dimensional statistics and combinatorics.

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from MIT advised by Elchanan Mossel. Before that I was an undergraduate at Princeton, where my thesis was advised by Allan Sly.
